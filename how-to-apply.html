How to Apply for Jobs

Follow the step-by-step guide below to apply for your desired job easily through Career Jobs.

  1. Go to the New Openings page to find available jobs.
  2. Read the job details carefully, including eligibility criteria and instructions.
  3. Click the “Apply Now” button under your chosen job.
  4. Login to your account or register as a new user.
  5. Fill in all the required information and upload necessary documents.
  6. Review your form carefully before submitting.
  7. After submission, you’ll receive a confirmation email or message.
